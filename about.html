About FlowNest

Helping students stay organised, focused, and in control of their workload.

FlowNest was created to address a common problem faced by university students: juggling multiple assignments, deadlines, and commitments across different subjects.

Our goal is to provide a simple, intuitive platform that helps students stay organised, plan their workload effectively, and reduce academic stress.

FlowNest is currently in development, with future plans including calendar integration, collaboration tools, and personalised productivity insights.
